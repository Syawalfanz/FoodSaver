How FoodSaver Works

For Buyers and Sellers — everything you need to know

For Buyers

3 Steps to Save & Eat

🔍

1. Discover

Browse surplus food listings near you. Filter by price, cuisine type, and pickup time. All listings are fresh and picked up the same day.

💳

2. Reserve & Pay

Add items to your cart and checkout securely via Stripe sandbox. Get an instant confirmation with your pickup window.

🌱

3. Collect & Earn

Head to the seller's location within the pickup window. Earn GreenPoints with every purchase and redeem for rewards.

For Sellers

Turn Surplus Into Revenue

📝

1. List Your Surplus

Log in as a seller and create a listing in under 2 minutes from your dashboard. Set price, quantity, and pickup time.

🔔

2. Get Notified

Receive real-time notifications when someone reserves your item. Manage all orders from your seller dashboard.

💰

3. Get Paid

Stripe processes payment automatically. Earnings deposited within 2 business days. FoodSaver takes 10% commission only on completed sales.

Security & Trust

We Take Safety Seriously

🔒

Firebase Auth

Secure login with email/password and Google OAuth. All sessions managed with encrypted tokens. No plaintext passwords stored.

💳

Stripe Payments

PCI-compliant payment processing. Card data never touches FoodSaver's servers — all tokenized by Stripe's sandbox API.

🌐

HTTPS & SSL

All data transmitted over encrypted HTTPS. API credentials stored as environment variables, never hardcoded in source code.
