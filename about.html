Our Mission

Fighting Food Waste Through Digital Innovation

Malaysia wastes approximately 17,000 tonnes of food every day. Much of it is surplus from restaurants, cafes, and home cooks — food still safe and delicious, but discarded due to lack of redistribution channels.

FoodSaver bridges that gap. By connecting food sellers with budget-conscious buyers, we create value on both ends: sellers recover costs, buyers access quality food at up to 70% off.

This platform directly supports SDG9 — Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure, building sustainable digital food infrastructure across Malaysia.
